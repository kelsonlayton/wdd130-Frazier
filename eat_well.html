Eat Well

Simple Dorm Recipes

  • Overnight Oats: Oats, milk, fruit — make it in your dorm fridge.
  • Microwave Veggie Bowl: Frozen veggies + rice + seasoning.
  • Mug Omelet: Eggs + cheese + spinach cooked in 2 minutes.

Smart Budget Shopping

Buying healthy food doesn’t need to be expensive. Tips:

  • Choose frozen fruits/veggies—they last longer.
  • Stock up on cheap proteins like eggs, peanut butter, and yogurt.
  • Buy store-brand whenever possible.

Why Nutrition Matters

Good nutrition improves energy, focus, mood, and overall health. Eating simple, whole foods throughout the day helps you feel better in class and study sessions.
